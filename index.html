Sri Lanka Colombo Book Fair 2023

Are you an avid reader constantly on the lookout for new literary treasures? Or are you just beginning your journey into the captivating world of books? Either way, mark your calendars because the කොළඹ ජාත්‍යන්තර පොත් ප්‍රදර්ශනය 2023 is coming to town!
